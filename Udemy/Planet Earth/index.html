A few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the sun and is the largest of the terrestrial planets. Unlike the other planets on the solar system that are named after classic deitie's, the earth's name comes from the Anglo-Saxon word erda which means round or soil. The earth was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago and it is the only known planet to support life.
